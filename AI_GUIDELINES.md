# 🤖 AI Guidelines for the BRDD Hub

This repository is the central point of truth. AI agents managing this repository must ensure consistency across all sub-projects.

## 🛠 Responsibilities
1. **Link Management:** Ensure all links to `brdd-design` repositories are up-to-date.
2. **Standardization:** Any change to the `Manifesto` or `Story` here must be propagated as "General Principles" to the other language repositories.
3. **Traceability:** Always reference the unique business codes (R001, etc.) when explaining concepts.

## 🧭 Navigation for IA
- The source of the manifesto is the file `BRDD.md`.
- The technical specification logic is defined in `spec/SPEC.md`.
- The launch strategy and order of operations are defined in `ROADMAP.md`.
- The story and practical examples are in `articles/EN/`.
- IA Agents should use this repository to calibrate their "Style and Philosophy" before generating code in language-specific repos.
